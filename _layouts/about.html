Hello there and welcome to my site! I am a cinematographer, photographer, and visual artist based in the San Francisco Bay Area.

With the rise in intersections of media, technology and art, I am super excited to see what kind of products I can make that push the bounds of creativity. I picked up my first camera (a Canon T3i) when trying to make the best of an involuntary family trip, and ever since I have been determined to document my adventures around the world. Outside of traveling, I am focused on taking my skills to a wider audience in commercial, short film, and other artistic applications. When not lugging around camera gear, you can catch me on the Tahoe slopes, at a concert somewhere, or jamming out as a bedroom DJ.

Please do not hesitate to contact me if you are interested in collaborating and working together!
