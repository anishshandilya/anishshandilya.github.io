Anish Shandilya is a cinematographer, photographer, visual artist and programmer based in the Bay Area. Given today's intersection of media, technology and art, Anish is proudly able to take on these many roles in order to make a product suitable by modern standards. He first picked up Canon T3i when trying to make the best of involuntary family trip, and ever since had been determined to document his adventures around the world. Now Anish is also focussed on expanding his skills and knowledge to a wider audience in commerical, short films and realtime artforms. When not lugging around camera gear, he can be found on the Tahoe slopes, playing pickup, or making trap/future bass tracks.
